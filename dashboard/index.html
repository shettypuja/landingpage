Bringing Industry to classroom

We are pioneers in designing and delivering industry-aligned programs to enhance the knowledge and skills of engineering students. We have partnered with leading global organizations to develop unique academic curricula that are aligned to the industry needs.


Our programs have demonstrated a significant increase in the employabilityof graduating students and have positioned them to pursue careers in diverse work areas such as research, product development, services, consulting and entrepreneurship. Our ecosystem helps students achieve higher levels of learning by accessing the most recent developments in their chosen areas of technology, thereby improving their ability to perform higher level tasks and increasing their employability.


Universities and colleges attract a larger number of students and get an opportunity to collaborate with state-of-the-art innovations and technologies.

B.Tech Program

4-year Extended Certification Parallel Program

CEO Message

Sujit has 20 years of work experience in the IT industry in the higher education space. Under his leadership, the company has developed an innovative industry-aligned B.Tech. program in partnership with IT Leaders to bridge the gap between academia and industry.


He architects the programs core capabilities such as curriculum design, course design, content creation, collaboration, class management system, problem resolution and mentor support among others.


He is also a part of the Joint Course Committee and is on the Advisory board of various universities. He is an alumnus of IIT Kanpur.


Industry associations: CII, TiE and NASSCOM.
